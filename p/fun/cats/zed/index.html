Welcome to Zed's planet!




Zed is a medium hair orange cat. He is very fluffy and sweet. He was adopted in Jan. 2017 from the ASPCA in Mesquite, TX. He is the only one of the five to be born a stray. He was scared of the streets so he turned himself in.



His number one concern is saftey. Zed will never stay anywhere that is unsafe. He always imagines the worst will happen.



If you are Ninja or Cassidy then Zed is nice to you. Everyone else gets the Zed glare.



Zed's favorite toy is his laser pointer. It is very safe to chase lasers.



Zed is a man of schedules. If we stay up late and don't put him to bed on time he will go upstairs and yell at us to put him to bed.



When he thinks he is alone, Zed likes to sing beautiful songs. He has the voice of an angel.



Thank you for visiting Zed's Planet!
