Research Interests

My main research interest is numerical methods for nematic liquid crystals, and other phase field models. Specifically, I have been working on building numerical methods for simulating nematic liquid crystals using a Q-tensor model. In addition, I have also worked on numerical methods for the Cahn-Hilliard phase field model.
